Job Description Summary


Oversee the operations of the University’s ICT department and ensure it aligns with the mission and vision of the university; administer and implement ICT policies and procedures; assume responsibility for the day-to-day control of the University’s Enterprise Resource Planner (ERP) system; plan, recommend and execute initiatives towards the future development of the University’s ICT resources and infrastructure; and guarantee the optimal performance of the University’s hardware and software.
